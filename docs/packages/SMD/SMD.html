 Package: SMD package 

SMD, or Surface-Mount Device, refers to electronic components designed to be mounted directly onto the surface of a printed circuit board rather than inserted through holes, allowing for much smaller, lighter, and more densely packed circuits. These components typically have flat leads or contact pads instead of long wire pins, enabling automated placement and soldering processes that improve manufacturing speed, reliability, and consistency.
