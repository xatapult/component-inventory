 Computer relay 

These are ancient computer relays. Simple, but fast in switching (not for very big loads).

They come with feet "arrays" of 4 x 5 relays, so you can put 20 together.

 

Coil voltage: 24 V DC ⓓ
Changeover contacts: 1 ⓓ
Coil impedance: 1500 Ω ⓓ
