 Category: SN74 

The SN74 series is a broad family of Texas Instruments logic ICs built on modern CMOS technology, offering fast switching speeds, low power consumption, and wide operating voltage ranges that make them suitable for both classic digital logic tasks and contemporary embedded designs. These devices maintain functional compatibility with earlier 7400-series logic while improving electrical performance, noise immunity, and efficiency. The SN74 lineup includes gates, flip-flops, counters, buffers, translators, and many other building-block functions, giving designers a flexible toolkit for constructing reliable digital systems across industrial, consumer, and automotive applications.
