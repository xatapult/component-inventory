 Relay Omron - Omron relay 24V DC (4C) 

  • To use the built-in LED: 13 = - and 14 = +
  • Pins 9 + 10 + 11 + 12 = common
  • Pin 4 (front row beside the coil pins 13 and 14) = NC of common pin 12
  • Pins 1 + 2 + 3 = NC of common pins 9 + 10 + 11
  • Pins 5 + 6 + 7 + 8 = NO
