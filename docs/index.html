Component-inventory home

I’m a tinkerer who uses electronic and electromechanical components to create bizarre and hopefully interesting installations and devices. I call it “Theatronics”: electronics with a theatrical twist. Examples of these can be found on the Erik’s Theatronics website.

Over the years, I’ve acquired a large number of electronic components, mostly through the acquisition of secondhand batches of parts. There have been so many of them that I’ve lost track a bit, hence this website. It also allows me to find additional information, such as datasheets, more easily.

There are so many now that I will probably never be able to use them all in my lifetime. I also don’t have an application for all components (I work more with digital than analogue).

 

Therefore, if you see something interesting or just want a few components for a very small price, let me know.

 

Of course, there may be additional packaging and shipping costs. I won’t sell all, but most of it is for sale. And that would do me a favor too: I’d rather have things used than left lying around in my shed!
